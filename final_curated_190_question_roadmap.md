## How to use this roadmap

For each question:

1. Write the algorithm or pseudocode first.
2. Solve it without copying a solution.
3. Dry-run it manually with at least three inputs.
4. Test boundary and invalid inputs.
5. Explain every condition and loop in your own words.
6. Re-solve missed questions after 2–3 days.

Do not move to Stage 2 until you can independently solve most of Stage 1. Do not treat Stage 3 as optional: it builds speed, syntax fluency, and confidence.
